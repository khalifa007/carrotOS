before you start unlock the r1 bootloader

shipped r1s are locked. you must unlock from rabbithole before any flash will succeed. this voids your warranty. rabbit support will not help you re-lock or recover.

flashing is at your own risk. carrotos and this flasher are community work — if your r1 bricks, won't boot, or loses cellular after flashing, we can't recover it for you. proceed only if you accept that.

  1. i sign in to hole.rabbit.tech ↗ — your r1 must be powered on + online for it to register.
  2. ii settings → developer. type the warranty acknowledgment, enter your imei (r1 → settings → about), tap void warranty and enable developer mode.
  3. iii same page · device modification → unlock. this authorizes the next fastboot unlock on rabbit's side.
  4. iv run rabbit's flash tool at rabbit-hmi-oss.github.io/flashing ↗ — it gets the r1 into fastboot and pushes fastboot flashing unlock for you.
  5. v bootloader is now unlocked. come back here. continue with begin below.
